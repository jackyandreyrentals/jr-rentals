Privacy Notice

Jacky & Rey Rentals is committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines.

📋 Who We Are (Data Controller)

Business Name: Jacky & Rey Rentals

Nature of Business: Event equipment and camping gear rental — tables, chairs, camping sets, and related items for events in Metro Cebu.

Contact: jackyandreyrentals@gmail.com | 09912094757 / 09500167754

Location: Metro Cebu, Philippines

As a Personal Information Controller (PIC) under RA 10173, we are responsible for the personal data you share with us when making bookings or inquiries.
📥 What Personal Data We Collect

When you use our booking or quotation services, we collect the following:

  • Identity information: Full name
  • Contact information: Phone number, email address
  • Location information: Event location, delivery address
  • Transaction information: Items rented, event dates, rental amount, payment method and reference
  • Digital signature: Drawn signature for rental contracts
  • Communication records: Notes and special requests you provide
🎯 Why We Collect Your Data (Purpose)

We collect and process your personal data solely for the following purposes:

  • To process and confirm your rental booking
  • To send booking confirmation and payment instructions to your email
  • To generate and manage your rental contract
  • To coordinate delivery and pickup of rental items
  • To track rental inventory and availability
  • To process payments and security deposit refunds
  • To contact you regarding your rental transaction
We will never use your personal data for marketing purposes without your explicit consent, and we will never sell your data to third parties.
🗄️ How We Store Your Data

Your personal data is stored in the following secure platforms:

  • Google Sheets — booking records, contact information, transaction details (protected by Google's security infrastructure)
  • Google Drive — signed rental contracts in PDF format (access-controlled, viewable only by you and J&R Rentals)
  • Gmail — email communications related to your booking

All platforms used are operated by Google LLC and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy and security standards. Data is not stored on any third-party server outside of Google's infrastructure.

⏱️ How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your personal data for two (2) years from the date of your last transaction with us. This is to comply with business record-keeping requirements and to handle any post-rental concerns such as damage claims or deposit refunds.

After the retention period, your data will be deleted from our records. You may also request earlier deletion (see Your Rights below).

🔗 Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except in the following limited circumstances:

  • Delivery partners — if you request delivery, your address and contact number may be shared with the delivery service (Lalamove, taxi, etc.) solely for the purpose of completing your delivery
  • Legal compliance — if required by law, court order, or government authority

In all cases, we only share the minimum necessary information.

⚖️ Your Rights Under RA 10173

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

📣 Right to be Informed
You have the right to know what personal data we collect and how it is used — which is the purpose of this Privacy Notice.
🔍 Right to Access
You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.
✏️ Right to Correction
You may request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold.
🗑️ Right to Erasure
You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to our legal retention obligations.
🚫 Right to Object
You may object to the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
📦 Right to Data Portability
You may request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:
📧 jackyandreyrentals@gmail.com
📞 09912094757 / 09500167754
We will respond to your request within 15 business days as required by the NPC.

🛡️ Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. These include:

  • Access to our booking system is password-protected (admin only)
  • Signed contracts are stored in access-controlled Google Drive folders
  • We do not store payment card information — payments are processed through GCash and MariBank directly
🔄 Changes to This Privacy Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will post the updated notice on this page with a revised date.

Continued use of our booking services after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Notice.

📮 Contact & Complaints

If you have concerns about how we handle your personal data, or if you believe your data privacy rights have been violated, you may:

  • Contact us directly at jackyandreyrentals@gmail.com
  • File a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) at privacy.gov.ph

📅 Day 2 onwards — each additional day is charged at the discounted rate
🧮 Formula: Total = (Day 1 price) + (Day 2+ price × remaining days)
